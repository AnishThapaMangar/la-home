"Orchid Tree"

Bauhinia blakeana is an evergreen tree that is found in various parts of the world. The tree belongs to Hong Kong and has been popular due to its beautiful pink flowers. The tree is also known as the Hong Kong orchid tree. This hybrid leguminous tree belongs to the genus Bauhinia. The tree is now cultivated in many countries around the world and planted for its ornamental nature. Additionally, it is used to make medicines for a variety of diseases. The Bauhinia Blakeana plant is found in the warm parts of California, Arizona, southern Florida and Texas.

The Bauhinia blakeana tree rises to a height of about 15-20 feet. It can grow even higher when it has surpassed the age of maturity. The leaves in the tree are large and thick.

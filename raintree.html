"Rain Tree"

Samanea saman(Rain Tree) is a species of flowering tree in the pea family,and is native to Central and South America. It is well represented in many languages and has numerous local names in its native range; common English names include saman, rain tree and monkeypod tree.The tree is grown to provide shade for other crops, including cocoa, coffee, tea and peppers.The long black pods and the leaves are rich in tannins and they are relished for livestock fodder.
